STEP THERAPY DOCUMENTATION
==========================

--- Trial 1 ---
Drug Name:              Mesalamine (Asacol HD)
Dose:                   800 mg three times daily
Start Date:             2024-03-01
End Date:               2024-08-15
Duration of Trial:      ~5.5 months
Outcome / Reason for Discontinuation:
  Inadequate response. Persistent symptoms throughout trial. Repeat CRP at 5 months
  remained elevated (38 mg/L). Patient reported no meaningful improvement in stool
  frequency or abdominal pain. Mesalamine is generally not effective for Crohn's
  colitis; trial completed per payer requirement.

--- Trial 2 ---
Drug Name:              Azathioprine
Dose:                   150 mg once daily
Start Date:             2024-09-01
End Date:               2025-03-20
Duration of Trial:      ~6.5 months
Outcome / Reason for Discontinuation:
  Adverse reaction. Patient developed drug-induced hepatotoxicity — ALT elevated to
  3x ULN at 5-month follow-up (2025-02-10). Drug discontinued per hepatology
  recommendation. TPMT enzyme level normal; idiosyncratic reaction suspected.

--- Trial 3 ---
Drug Name:              Budesonide (Entocort EC)
Dose:                   9 mg once daily, tapered to 6 mg
Start Date:             2025-04-01
End Date:               2025-07-01
Duration of Trial:      3 months
Outcome / Reason for Discontinuation:
  Temporary response with relapse on taper. Symptoms returned to baseline within
  4 weeks of dose reduction. Corticosteroid-dependent pattern confirmed. Long-term
  steroid use not appropriate; escalation to biologic therapy warranted.
