STEP THERAPY DOCUMENTATION
==========================

--- Trial 1 ---
Drug Name:              Metformin
Dose:                   1000 mg twice daily
Start Date:             2021-06-01
End Date:               (ongoing — still on medication)
Duration of Trial:      4+ years
Outcome / Reason for Discontinuation:
  Partial response. HbA1c improved from 11.2% to 8.6% in first year but has since
  risen to 9.8%. Metformin continued as background therapy per guidelines; additional
  agent required.

--- Trial 2 ---
Drug Name:              Sitagliptin (Januvia)
Dose:                   100 mg once daily
Start Date:             2023-01-10
End Date:               (ongoing — still on medication)
Duration of Trial:      3+ years
Outcome / Reason for Discontinuation:
  Inadequate response as add-on to metformin. HbA1c reduction of only 0.4% observed
  (from 10.2% to 9.8%). DPP-4 inhibitors have modest efficacy; insufficient for this
  patient's degree of hyperglycemia.

--- Trial 3 ---
Drug Name:              Empagliflozin (Jardiance)
Dose:                   10 mg once daily
Start Date:             2025-01-15
End Date:               2025-05-01
Duration of Trial:      3.5 months
Outcome / Reason for Discontinuation:
  Adverse reaction. Patient developed three episodes of urinary tract infection
  within 3 months, one requiring oral antibiotics. SGLT-2 inhibitor mechanism
  increases glucosuria, predisposing to genitourinary infections. Discontinued
  due to recurrent adverse events.
